About Rachael Ray

Rachael Ray was born August 25, 1968. She is an American television personality, businesswoman, celebrity cook, and author. She hosts a daily talk and lifestyle program Rachael Ray, and three Food Network series. Ray has written several cookbooks based on the 30 Minute Meals concept, and launched a magazine Every Day with Rachael Ray. Ray's television shows have won Emmy Awards. (source: wikepedia)
